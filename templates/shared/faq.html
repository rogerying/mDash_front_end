Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help you?

What is a Dash?

A Dash is a digital cryptographically secured fingerprint of an item that’s secured that’s embedded within a link. When a user click’s the link their item is realized into their Amazon account via secured cryptography to unlock the item.

Can we refunds?

Yes, refunds will take 2-4 weeks to process and charge 5-10% of remaining amount.

How do clients claim items?

Clients will reach a landing page, where they fill in their details (including security measures), they are securely sent a link to their claim page via e-mail, where they can claim the item.

How many items can a person claim per campaign?

At this time, each campaign is restricted to one Amazon account per campaign.

What security measures can I put in place?

You can make redemption codes (unique or same code) for your users to claim OR only make it that only e-mail addresses set by you can claim the rewards. We will be rolling out different ways to claim, including SMS etc… Furthermore, we have added [cyber security measures] and are GDPR compliant and understand your data privacy is key.

What if prices change on items in Amazon?

Your client will have a credit for that item for the amount that was paid by you. e.g. if you credit him with $50, but there was a price change to $55, your client will have to pay $5 extra to claim the item. Alternatively, if the price goes to $45, then your client will have an extra $5 credit on the item.

Can I cover the shipping and taxes on the item for my client?

Unfortunately at this moment, no. Due to not being able to control where clients want their items to be shipped (local, overseas, shipping options), we recommend only covering the cost of the product.

What if the item I want is not fulfilled by Amazon?

You would have to make sure that the 3rd Party seller on the marketplace has enough items in stock to fulfill your campaign.

What if Amazon runs out of stock of an item?

Your clients will still have a credit for the item, and once the item is back in stock then he or she can ship the item.

Why are there different charges in payments in credit card and bank transfer?

Due to the different charges in payment gateways, some charge less than others for different forms of payments, unfortunately these costs are unavoidable.

How long will it take for my order to be ready?

Right now, in beta version, we estimate between 1-5 days, we will improve upon this in the near future.
